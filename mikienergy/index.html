About Miki-Controls Clean Energy.

MIKI CONRTOLS FUELESS GENERATOR is a Green Renewable Energy Product Development, Marketing Company that promotes green renewable energy inventions togovernments, public utility, banking institutions, unions and consumers globally.

As electricity is entwined with every aspects of day-to-day life, the issue of reliablity is paramount. Miki-Controls Clean Energy is here to prevent inconvenience experienced by consumers during prolonged periods without electricity with quality soundless and fuelless generator that does not produce toxic air pollutants during use. Our Generators can also function as a standalone energy source for mining companies, medical facilities, research centers, construction firms, as well as other industrial and commercial enterprises operating in remote locations.

MIKI CONTROLS ENERGY Installation

Miki Controls Energy personnel will be present for installation in order to ensure units are properly engaged and generating electricity. Service and maintenance operations will be catered to the specific needs of individual clients and will outlined in a maintenance contract. Although technical issues are rare, simple technical problems can be fixed by consulting our troubleshooting guide. Alternatively, our trained technicians can provide over the phone service to solve issues that may arise. In some cases, especially in remote locations, it may be necessary to consult a local technician. We offer an extensive training program and educational seminars to a local team so that they can provide maintenance and repairs on our fuelless generator units when needed. The generator will be produced and delivered according to the Voltage requirement, grid specification, amperage and hertz requirement of each country. The location of installation use of the independent Generator MFG is possible in any region inside the country. If necessary or by emergency, it can even be transported from one location to another and/or used as a mobile generator.
